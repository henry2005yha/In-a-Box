Chorizo Chili

with Shredded Cheese & Scallion Sour Cream

  • Saturated Fat 20g

Ingredients

ground beef, ground pork, chorizo (pork, spices (black pepper, chili pepper, cinnamon, clove, coriander, cumin, oregano, red pepper), salt, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, garlic, onion, white vinegar, natural hickory smoke flavor, natural flavoring), water, sour cream (cultured cream), shredded pepper jack cheese ((pasteurized milk, jalapeño peppers, cheese culture, salt, enzymes), potato starch and powdered cellulose added to prevent caking, natamycin (a natural mold inhibitor)), tomato paste (tomatoes, citric acid), shredded cheddar cheese (pasteurized milk, cheese cultures, salt, enzymes, annatto vegetable color, anti-caking agent (potato starch, powdered cellulose, corn starch, natamycin)), green bell peppers, tomato sauce (tomatoes, tomato puree, salt, red pepper, dehydrated bell peppers, dehydrated onion, dehydrated garlic, onion powder, garlic powder, spice extractive, citric acid, natural flavoring), yellow bell peppers, onions, green onions, garlic, cilantro, chili powder (chili pepper, spices, salt, garlic powder), sea salt, ancho chile powder, cumin

Preparation Instruction

As easy as heat and eat!

HEATING OPTION 1 - MICROWAVE

HEATING TIMES MAY VARY; REHEAT CONTENTS TO 165°F.

1.Remove outer packaging and pierce plastic film a few times with a fork or sharp knife to vent. Remove cup. 2. Microwave on HIGH for 2 minutes. If needed, continue to heat in 30 second intervals until desired temperature is reached. 3. Let stand for 2 minutes. Carefully remove film. Transfer contents to a plate and enjoy!

HEATING OPTION 2 - CONVENTIONAL OVEN

1. Adjust rack to middle position and preheat oven to 375°F. 2. Remove outer packaging and plastic film. Remove cup; add 2 oz water to empty compartment. 3. Place tray on a baking sheet and bake for 7 minutes. If needed, continue to bake for 2-4 more minutes or until desired temperature is reached. 4. Transfer contents to a plate and enjoy!
